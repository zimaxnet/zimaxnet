Enhanced Prompting System Revolution

Orb Game features the world's most sophisticated prompting system for AI-powered educational content with 180+ model-specific prompts across 4 AI models (o4-mini, grok-4, perplexity-sonar, gemini-1.5-flash), 8 categories, 5 historical epochs, and 2 languages with cultural sensitivity.

Bilingual Excellence with Cultural Sensitivity

Orb Game provides full bilingual support with English and Spanish content that feels natural and engaging, not just translations. With 360 total prompts (180 English + 180 Spanish), the platform offers seamless language switching with automatic content refresh and appropriate cultural context for Spanish-speaking users worldwide.
