How Zimax AIMCS handles data and works with integrated systems

Zimax AIMCS activity and your choices

When you use Zimax AIMCS, we collect your activity, including your conversations, files and data you share (such as documents, images, and system outputs), usage patterns, your feedback, and relevant context information. This data is processed through Azure AI services, reviewed by authorized personnel when necessary, and used to improve and personalize your Zimax AIMCS experience.

You can review & manage your activity and control how it is used anytime in your Zimax AIMCS Dashboard. Please don't enter sensitive information you wouldn't want processed or reviewed.

Review & Manage Activity Learn More

Zimax AIMCS can integrate with your systems

Zimax AIMCS can respond with real-time information from connected services and systems through our Model Context Protocol integration. You can choose to connect services like Azure services, GitHub repositories, and your organization's approved systems.

When Zimax AIMCS identifies a connected system that can provide relevant information, it will send necessary context from your conversation to that system to generate better, more informed responses. You can manage your connected systems and permissions in your integration settings.
